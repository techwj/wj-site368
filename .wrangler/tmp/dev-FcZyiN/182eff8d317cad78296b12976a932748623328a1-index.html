Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI QR Code Generator?

Our AI QR Code Generator creates functional QR codes with beautiful AI-generated backgrounds. Unlike plain black and white codes, ours combine functionality with stunning visual design.

How do I use the QR code generator?

Simply enter your website URL, choose a background style, and click "Generate QR Code". The tool will create a scannable QR code with an AI-generated artistic background.

Are the generated QR codes scannable?

Yes! Despite having beautiful backgrounds, all our QR codes maintain full scannability. We ensure proper contrast and positioning so any QR code scanner can read them.

Can I use these QR codes commercially?

Absolutely! You can use the generated QR codes for any purpose including commercial use, marketing materials, business cards, menus, and promotional campaigns.

How can I share the QR codes?

You can download the QR codes as high-resolution images and share them anywhere - social media, print materials, websites, or emails. They work on all devices and platforms.

What background styles are available?

We offer multiple AI-generated background styles including artistic, modern, gradient, and themed designs. Each style is carefully crafted to maintain QR code readability.

Is the service free to use?

Yes! Our AI QR Code Generator is completely free to use. Generate as many beautiful QR codes as you need without any cost or registration requirements.

What websites can I create QR codes for?

You can create QR codes for any valid website URL - personal websites, business pages, social media profiles, online stores, menus, portfolios, and more.
